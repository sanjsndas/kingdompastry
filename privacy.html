Privacy Policy

We treat your data like a heritage apple — with care, respect, and never for purposes beyond the orchard.

📅 Last updated: 15 July 2026

Our commitment

At Kingdom Pastry Orchard, we believe that privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or interact with us. We are guided by the principles of transparency, minimal data collection, and orchard ethics.

🍂 The short version: We only collect what we need to respond to your inquiries or improve our content. We never sell your data, and we use cookies only to enhance your experience. You are always in control.

1. Information we collect

We collect minimal information, only when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact form: name, email address, and any message you send.
  • Newsletter sign-up: email address (only if you opt in).
  • Cookies: we use essential cookies for functionality and analytics to understand which pages are most loved by our readers.

2. How we use your data

  • To reply to your messages and inquiries.
  • To send occasional updates about orchard events, new recipes, or seasonal stories (only if you have given explicit consent).
  • To improve our website and content based on anonymous analytics.

3. Data storage & security

Your data is stored securely on protected servers. We use industry-standard encryption and regularly review our security practices. We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

4. Your rights

  • Access: you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: you can update or correct your information at any time.
  • Deletion: you can ask us to delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent: you can unsubscribe from our emails at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact our orchard keepers — we’re here to help.

5. Cookies & tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies to ensure the site works properly and to understand how visitors interact with our content. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

6. Third-party services

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use trusted services (such as email delivery platforms) that comply with strict data protection standards. All processors are bound by confidentiality agreements.

7. Children’s privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

📬 Questions? We’re happy to clarify anything. Reach us at privacy@kingdompastryorchard.com or through our contact page.
