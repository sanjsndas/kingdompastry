Cookies Policy

Like a perfect shortbread, our cookies are simple, transparent, and made to enhance your experience — never to crumble your privacy.

🍪 Last updated: 15 July 2026

Our cookie philosophy

At Kingdom Pastry Orchard, we use cookies with the same care we use when selecting heirloom apples. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage them. We keep things minimal and respectful.

🍂 The short version: We only use essential and analytics cookies. No tracking for advertising, no selling of data. You can disable cookies anytime in your browser settings.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and ensure the site works properly. Think of them as tiny orchard keepers — they help us tend to your experience.

How we use cookies

We use cookies exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Essential: to keep the website secure and functional (e.g., session management).
  • Analytics: to understand which pages are most popular and how visitors navigate the orchard. We use aggregated, anonymized data.
  • Preferences: to remember your settings (e.g., dark mode preference, if implemented).

We do not use cookies for advertising, remarketing, or any form of cross-site tracking.

Cookies we use

Below is a clear list of the cookies we set and what they do. We believe in full transparency.

Name Type Purpose Duration
session_id Essential Maintains your session while you browse the site. Session
preferences Preference Stores your preferred language or display settings. 1 year
analytics_visitor Analytics Helps us count visits and traffic sources (anonymized). 2 years
cookie_consent Essential Remembers your cookie preference (to avoid showing the banner again). 1 year

Manage your cookies

You are in control. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the website. You can adjust your preferences at any time in your browser settings.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data.
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies.

Third-party services

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Our analytics are powered by a privacy-first service that does not collect IP addresses or personal identifiers. We never share your data with advertisers.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or regulation. Any updates will be posted here with a revised date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
